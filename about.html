What is the CanAm Missing Project?

The CanAm Missing Project started when David Paulides was doing research in a National Park and an off-duty park ranger expressed his concerns about the number of people that had gone missing in the parks under strange circumstances.

After Paulides started his research for that park, he felt he had uncovered mysterious series of mass disappearances all over the world. These people, including the three listed on the home page, all disappeared under very strange circumstances. Most were avid and experienced outdoorsman and women who were there one second, gone the next. Some were never seen again, not even their belongings. Some had their remains found and some were even found alive but didn't remember why they were out there and even what had happened to them while they were gone.
